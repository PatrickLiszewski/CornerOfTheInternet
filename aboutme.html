About Me

Hello! I'm Patrick Liszewski, a passionate computer science education student at Siena College! Welcome to my personal space on the web!

Aside from computers, I also enjoy theater and video games, see more about my interests on my blog!

My Background

I was born in New Jersey, raised in New York. Although I was expected to be the athletic kid of the family I somehow drifted towards theater and found a passion there.

My love of technology was created early on as a result of the relationship I had with my older sibling, someone from the start of the computer era, where to have a social media presence you had to know html and make the page yourself.

They gifted me an immense respect for what technology can do (and also got me hooked on video games), and here I am all these years later striving to be a Computer Science Educator!
